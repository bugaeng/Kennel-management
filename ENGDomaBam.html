Environmental Management System



Site Purpose


Checking the temperature, moisture, and other environmental conditions by yourself is a hassle and a tedious task.

We provide a service to check the temperature, moisture, and lighting data using sensors and data processing methods to support reptile breeding.

You can remotely adjust the environment while doing other tasks on your computer.









Introduction



Lizard and other reptiles and special animals are sensitive to environmental conditions such as temperature and humidity.

Depending on the individual and species, reptiles need a swamp-like or desert-like environment to survive without stress.

To support this, our website provides control capability using a control panel that can adjust temperature and humidity through the use of a fan and LED lighting.



Supported Systems



Temperature

The temperature value data is read from the temperature sensor installed in the breeding facility.

You can output the data on the web to check the temperature of the breeding facility.

You don't need to look at the thermometer one by one on site.



Moisture

You can adjust humidity to the optimal level.

Setting the optimal condition for humidity, around 50-80% depending on the individual, and species and check if the humidity values are within the designated range.



LED

You can adjust LED brightness according to the temperature in the breeding facility.

The automatic control function is available, and as the temperature increases, the brightness automatically decreases.



Development Direction

The goal is to automate the operation of fan and humidifier by reading temperature, humidity and LED values.

Precautions When Using


We are not responsible for malfunctions and damages caused by user carelessness.

Please be careful not to let fans touch children's hands.

As bacteria can breed, please change and sterilize the water once a day in the humidifier.

As electrical components are installed, do not spray water or immerse in water to avoid electric shock.
